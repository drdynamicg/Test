Copyright Policy

AniWall respects intellectual property rights. If you believe a wallpaper infringes your copyright, please submit a report using the “Report this wallpaper” button on the wallpaper page. Provide:

  • Your contact information
  • Identification of the copyrighted work
  • The URL of the infringing wallpaper

We will investigate and remove infringing content promptly.

For questions, email copyright@aniwall.com.
