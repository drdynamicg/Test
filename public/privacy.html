Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2025

At AniWall, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you log in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. We store this information to identify you when you upload wallpapers or submit reports.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your email is used only to attribute uploads and to contact you regarding your submissions or reports. We never sell or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use a session cookie (HTTP‑only) to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies for analytics or advertising outside of Google AdSense, which may set its own cookies.

4. Data Security

All data is stored securely on Cloudflare servers. Your password is never stored because you log in via Google OAuth.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated uploads by contacting us at admin@aniwall.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance.
